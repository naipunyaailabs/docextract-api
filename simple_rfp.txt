REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL
====================

RFP Number: RFP-2023-001
Title: IT Services for Digital Transformation
Issue Date: October 1, 2023
Response Due Date: November 15, 2023

1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
-------------------
The City of Springfield seeks to modernize its IT infrastructure to improve citizen services and operational efficiency.

2. SCOPE OF WORK
---------------
The selected vendor will:
- Assess current IT systems
- Design and implement cloud-based solutions
- Provide staff training
- Offer ongoing technical support

3. SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------
Proposals must include:
- Technical approach
- Project timeline
- Cost breakdown
- Team qualifications

4. EVALUATION CRITERIA
---------------------
Proposals will be evaluated based on:
- Technical approach (40%)
- Cost competitiveness (30%)
- Past performance (20%)
- Project timeline (10%)
