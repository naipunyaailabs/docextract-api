Request for Proposal: Mobile App Development

Introduction

StartUp Innovations is seeking a qualified vendor to develop a mobile application for our food delivery service. This Request for Proposal (RFP) outlines the requirements and expectations for the development of the mobile app.

Project Overview

We are looking for a vendor to design, develop, and deploy a mobile application for our food delivery service. The app should provide a seamless user experience, allowing customers to easily order food from various restaurants, track their orders in real-time, and make payments securely.

Scope of Work

The scope of this project includes:

  • Design and development of a mobile application for iOS and Android platforms
  • Integration with our existing payment gateway
  • Implementation of real-time order tracking
  • Testing and quality assurance
  • Deployment and maintenance

Technical Requirements

The mobile application must meet the following technical requirements:

  • Available on both iOS and Android platforms
  • Support real-time tracking of orders
  • Integrate with our payment gateway
  • Ensure data security and integrity
  • Provide a user-friendly interface

Budget Information

Our budget for this project is $75,000. We request that vendors provide detailed pricing for all components and services, including:

  • Development costs
  • Testing and quality assurance costs
  • Deployment and maintenance costs
  • Any other costs associated with the project

Proposal Guidelines

Vendors are requested to submit their proposals in the following format:

  • Company overview and experience in mobile app development
  • Description of the proposed solution and technical approach
  • Project timeline and milestones
  • Detailed pricing and cost breakdown
  • References and case studies

Evaluation Criteria

Proposals will be evaluated based on the following criteria:

  • Vendor experience and expertise in mobile app development
  • Technical approach and solution fit
  • Project timeline and milestones
  • Price and value for money
  • References and case studies

Submission Deadline

Proposals must be submitted by November 15, 2025 to be considered.
