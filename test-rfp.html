Website Development RFP

Issued by: Tech Corp

Deadline: 2025-12-31

Introduction

Tech Corp is seeking a qualified vendor to design and develop a new website that will serve as a central hub for our online presence. The goal of this project is to create a modern, user-friendly, and responsive website that effectively communicates our brand and services to our target audience.

This Request for Proposal (RFP) outlines the requirements and expectations for the project. Vendors are invited to submit their proposals, including a detailed description of their approach, methodology, and pricing.

Project Scope

The scope of this project includes the design and development of a new website, including:

  • Custom design that aligns with our brand guidelines
  • Responsive layout for optimal viewing on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
  • Content management system (CMS) integration
  • Homepage, about us, services, and contact pages
  • Integration with social media platforms
  • Basic SEO optimization

The objectives of this project are to:

  • Improve our online presence and brand awareness
  • Enhance user experience and engagement
  • Increase website traffic and conversion rates

Technical Requirements

The website must meet the following technical specifications:

  • Built on a scalable and secure CMS platform (e.g. WordPress, Drupal)
  • Compatible with the latest versions of Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, and Microsoft Edge
  • Optimized for search engines using HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript
  • Integrated with Google Analytics for tracking and analysis

Submission Requirements

Vendors must submit their proposals electronically in PDF format to proposal@techcorp.com by 2025-12-31.

Proposals must include:

  • Company overview and experience in website development
  • Project approach and methodology
  • Detailed breakdown of costs and pricing
  • Portfolio of relevant work samples
  • References from previous clients

Evaluation Criteria

Proposals will be evaluated based on the following criteria:

Criteria Weight
Company experience and qualifications 20%
Project approach and methodology 30%
Pricing and value 20%
Portfolio and references 30%

Timeline

The project timeline is as follows:

Milestone Deadline
Proposal submission 2025-12-31
Vendor selection 2026-01-15
Project kickoff 2026-01-20
Design and development 2026-03-31
Testing and launch 2026-04-30

Terms and Conditions

The following terms and conditions apply to this RFP:

  • All proposals become the property of Tech Corp
  • Tech Corp reserves the right to reject any or all proposals
  • The selected vendor will be required to sign a contract outlining the scope of work, payment terms, and other requirements

Budget

The budget for this project is $50,000. Vendors are requested to provide a detailed breakdown of their costs and pricing.
